# Lemonade

This recipe is the result of having a bunch of unused lemons left over from making limoncello.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | 
| ------------- | ----------- |
| lemons | 11 | 
| Splenda | 10 packets |
| water | 2 1/4 cups | 
| ice | 2 cups | 

## Instructions

* Juice all lemons, straining pulp and seeds
* Pour juice into pitcher
* Add water
* Add Splenda
* Add ice
* Stir until Splenda is disolved
